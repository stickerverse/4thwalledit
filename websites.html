Frequently asked questions

What is the number 1 website builder?

There are many great websites for selling merchandise. We think Fourthwall is the best online store builder. It's the only one that gives you: 

  • Amazing quality products
  • Drag & drop designs
  • A no-code ecommerce website builder

All-in-one, so you don't have to compromise or install multiple tools. And Fourthwall does customer support on your behalf.

  • Printful (usually with Shopify)
  • Printify (usually with Shopify)
  • Spreadshop (Spreadshirt)
  • Sellfy
  • Redbubble
How can I create my own website for free?

There are no monthly fees, no upfront costs, and no contracts to use Fourthwall to create the best ecommerce website. Users set their own margins for selling online. Here's how our pricing & splits work:

  • Memberships: 5% flat fee
  • Digital products: 3% flat fee
  • Physical products: No fees! The manufacturing cost of a product from our product catalog (which you can monitor during the design process) gets deducted from the selling price you set, so you're in full control of your margins.

All transactions include an added 2.9% + $0.30 payment processing fee (same as Patreon & Shopify).

Can a beginner build a website?

With platforms like Fourthwall, creating the best ecommerce website to sell your products is as easy as:

‍

  1. Selecting the products that you want to sell online
  2. Uploading your designs (if you choose to use the Fourthwall catalog)
  3. Building your shop using our ecommerce site builder
  4. Start promoting and selling

No inventory is required to sell many custom products like shirts or hoodie. Fourthwall also includes a professional mockup generator to get pixel-perfect results for quality before you start selling.

Can an online store make money?

Yes absolutely! You can make money by creating your own online store and choosing to sell:

  • Items from the Fourthwall catalog
  • Self-fulfilled products
  • Digital products

With ecommerce platforms like Fourthwall, you can create your online store, customize products, and grow your brand - which helps you make money!
