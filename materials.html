Be inspired by our wide range of materials

When it comes to creating custom stickers, the material you choose is just as important as the design itself. Vinyl is a popular option for its durability and waterproof properties.

Whether you want a glossy finish for extra vibrancy, or a matte finish for a classy look and feel, our laminates not only look great but also stand up to weather and wear over time.

For a more eco-friendly and rustic choice opt for Kraft paper which is made out of 100% recycled brown paper.

Clear stickers are perfect for adding a subtle touch to any surface or window, and holographic options provide a fun and unique flair. Whatever material you choose it's easy to showcase your personality and style.

Which Sticker Material Is Right for You?

Make the right decision from the start – we’ll guide you along the way

Picking the right material for your custom stickers can feel daunting - but don’t worry, we’ve got your back! Whether you need durable labels for outdoor use, eye-catching packaging accents, or personal stickers for laptops, bottles, or gift boxes, we have the perfect sticker materials for your needs.

We offer everything from weatherproof vinyl and laminates to glittery, holographic, matte, and paper-feel options. No matter what you're planning, we’ll help you get the right feel, the right durability, and just the right amount of wow-factor to make sure whatever you slap them on truly pops. Popular sticker material uses among our customers include:

  • Matte and glossy finishes available for high-quality brand stickers

  • Clear vinyl - ideal for product packaging with a clean, professional look

  • Outdoor-grade materials - resistant to water, sunlight, and scratches

  • Removable vinyl - suitable for events, tech surfaces, or temporary applications

  • Eco-friendly paper options - perfect for sustainable packaging solutions
