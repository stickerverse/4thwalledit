Size and material options: Labels for every use

We print custom labels in almost any shape and form you require; round, oval, square, or contour cut according to your design. To ensure efficient production and minimize excess materials, we’ve standardized six different roll sizes based on label size.

Online, we are initially prioritizing our most popular material options and will continue to gradually introduce others. Current online options are White, Transparent, Silver, and recycled Kraft Paper. We regularly print special orders besides our online options. Reach out to us with any specific questions or requests.

Cost effective labelling: easy and convenient

Our flexible and advanced production capabilities empower us to offer not only highly competitive pricing for small orders of labels on roll, but also to extend discounts for higher volumes. We cater to both small and medium size businesses as well as individuals and artists.

Labels on a roll come with a convenient backing material that allows for easy peeling and application. Crafted from thinner, high-quality materials, our labels offer a sleek and professional appearance: specifically designed to be peeled off the roll smoothly and applied with ease.

FAQs Custom Labels

Here are some common questions on how to make and order custom labels.

The number of labels on each roll depends on the size of your order and the size of your labels. Contact us for specific information at info@stickerapp.com.

Labels can be round, oval, square, rectangle, or contour cut. Sizes range from 1 to 11 inches in width, with six standardized roll sizes.

Stickers are durable and eye-catching for branding. Labels are thinner, removable, and great for product info and packaging. Different tools for different jobs!

Prices are available in our wizard. Select the shape, size, quantity, and a few more options, and we’ll show you the price directly. For special requests, contact info@stickerapp.com.

Our online service supplies White, Transparent, and Silver Metallic, as well as recycled Kraft Paper. Contact us at info@stickerapp.com for more details and special requests.

Regular turnaround time is 1-2 days for production and 1-3 days for transport, depending on order size, type, and location. Estimated delivery times are shown at checkout.
