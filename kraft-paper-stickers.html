FAQ Kraft Paper Stickers

Looking for answers? Check out these related questions or dive into our support section for more insights.

Kraft paper stickers are less durable due to the absence of a laminate, best for indoor use like bullet journals or labels. They are ideal for writing on and eco-friendly projects.

Kraft paper stickers lack a protective laminate and are unsuitable for outdoor use. For weather-resistant options, choose our durable, UV-resistant materials.

Bleed is an extra margin around the design that extends beyond the final cut size, usually about 2 mm (0.08 inches), ensuring complete coverage even if cutting is slightly off.

Custom stickers can be as large as 48 inches (120 cm) on one side.
