Frequently asked questions

What are the 4 steps to create an app?
  1. Sign up for Fourthwall and enable Memberships
  2. Customize your site with our no-code design tools and custom premade themes
  3. Start creating content you love, and promote it to your audience
  4. Your app will automatically be created, so all your supporters need to do is add it to their home screen! 

For further instructions on customization and supporter access, head to our help center article.

Can anyone create an application?

Yes! Anyone and everyone with Memberships enabled on their Fourthwall site can make an app. You won’t need any app building software or any type of coding for this. Your selected site colors, fonts, favicon, and other settings will be used to generate a beautiful, personalized mobile app experience for your members.

Is it easy to build an app?

On other platforms, it may be challenging to make an app. However, with Fourthwall’s no-code app creation, it’s extremely easy to build an app. In fact, there’s no extra work needed on your end. Once you’ve created a site and enabled memberships, our platform will automatically build an app for you. Your supporters will see a pop-up to download the app once they become members.

How much does it cost to create an app?

It costs $0 to join Fourthwall, and it costs $0 to make an app on Fourthwall. This is a complementary feature that you can access once you’ve enabled Memberships. With our app builder, you’re able to bypass the 30% Apple and Google app fee. Since this will not be a “regular” app, your supporters will add the app to their home screen via their phone browser rather than the App Store. 

Fourthwall has a 95/5 revenue split (solely for Memberships) which is lower than other platforms.

What is the best free website to create an app without coding?

There are many great app builder options, but we believe that Fourthwall’s seamless mobile app maker is the best way to create an app without coding. Your content and product offerings will automatically be optimized for a phone app, with no extra work for you. Plus, you’ll be able to send push notifications for new content when your supporters become subscribers and download your app.
