Frequently asked questions

What is the best selling merchandise for bands?

Popular merchandise for bands typically includes t-shirts, hoodies, posters, and vinyl records. Limited edition items and exclusive designs often sell well, as they cater to the collector's market. With Fourthwall, you can explore a range of products to see what resonates best with your fans and supporters.

How do I set up a merch store for my band?

Setting up a merch store for your band on Fourthwall is simple:

1. Create Your Account: Sign up and set up your Fourthwall account.

2. Customize Your Site: Use our no-code custom website builder to personalize your online store. You can adjust colors, fonts, add images, and more to reflect your band's style.

3. Create Your Products: Select from a variety of manufacturing techniques like DTG, DTFx, embroidery, or screen-printing and upload your designs to start selling your merch.

Do bands make money on merch?

Yes, selling merchandise can be a significant source of revenue for bands. Merchandise not only provides an income stream but also helps in promoting your band's brand and connecting with fans on a personal level. Fourthwall enables you to keep a large portion of the sales profits, allowing you to maximize your earnings from merchandise sales.
