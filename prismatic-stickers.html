Perfectly prismatic

Give your stickers a shiny retro effect with our prismatic sticker material. This material is a mosaic of small holographic squares that change appearance in different angles of light. Our prismatic material also gives off a bit of a 3D effect, adding some dimension to your stickers. This material is holographic but a little more eye-catching since it's a whole bunch of small holographic squares.

A durable disco effect

Create prismatic stickers for all purposes. This material when applied for outdoor use will catch some crazy rainbow reflections from the natural daylight. Alternatively, this material can catch anyone's eye no matter the time of day when applied indoors. No matter your purpose any sticker with this material will turn heads.

FAQ Prismatic Stickers

Looking for answers? Check out these related questions or dive into our support section for more insights.

Prismatic material is composed of reflective rectangles that create a rainbow shine, ideal for highlighting specific parts of your sticker design.

Prismatic material features reflective rectangles for an '80s aesthetic, while glitter material has smaller overlapping circles. Both create a rainbow effect but with different visual textures.

Currently, we print each sticker design on a single material. This means that mixing different materials within the same sticker design is impossible
