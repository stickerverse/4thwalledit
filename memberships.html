Frequently asked questions

What integrations does Fourthwall have?

Fourthwall supports many free integrations, including:

  • Members-only Discord roles
  • Live gifting on Twitch for memberships & products
  • Custom discounts for Twitch subs
  • Personalized videos to send to your supporters
  • YouTube Merch Shelf
  • StreamElements and Streamlabs stream alerts
  • Facebook and Instagram Shopping
  • Members-only Discord server
  • TikTok feeds
  • and many more...
How much does it cost to use Fourthwall?

There are no monthly fees, no upfront costs, and no contracts to use Fourthwall. Creators set their own margins. Here's how our pricing & splits work:

  • Memberships: 5% flat fee
  • Digital products: 3% flat fee
  • Physical products: no fees The manufacturing cost of a product from our product catalog (which you can monitor during the design process) gets deducted from the selling price you set, so you're in full control of your margins.
  • All transactions include an added 2.9% + $0.30 payment processing fee (same as Patreon & Shopify).
Are there any requirements to join Fourthwall?

Fourthwall is focused on giving creators an exceptional support experience and product experience. To maintain this level of quality, we ask that creators meet any of the following basic requirements to get instant access to Fourthwall:

  • Twitch. 100+ followers and a 3+ month old account
  • YouTube. 500+ subscribers and a 3+ month old account
  • Twitter. 1000+ followers and a 3+ month old account

If you qualify for any of the above, you can click the Start for free button above to start instantly. For creators that do not qualify for instant access or have a different primary channel, you can Request an invite and our team will get back to you as quickly as possible.

Can I order samples?

Yes! Fourthwall helps you to launch an elevated high-quality product line that fits your brand. Whether that is retail-quality merch, bespoke goods, or something else, you should be proud of what you're selling to your fans. That's why we make it easy to order samples directly within the product experience, so you're 100% confident that you're giving your community a product that represents the same level of quality that you put into everything else that you do.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! Fourthwall works with manufacturing & fulfillment partners around the globe in the US, UK, EU, and AUS. Shipping rates are dynamically determined by the size of package and destination. We work with most major carriers and pass through the true cost of shipping. That means that you can offer low-cost, fast shipping to your fans. Most items have a delivery window of 7-10 days.

Be sure to browse our product catalog to find products that are fulfilled out of your target regions to provide the fastest & cheapest shipping for your community.
